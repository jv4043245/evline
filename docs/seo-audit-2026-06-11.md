# EVLine SEO audit - 2026-06-11

## What is good

- The site now has two clear organic funnels: parts from China and BYD programming.
- Ukrainian and Russian versions have canonical/hreflang pairs.
- The parts cluster covers brand intent: BYD, ZEEKR, Xiaomi, Li Auto, NIO, XPENG, AITO, AVATR, Denza and other Chinese brands.
- Model pages exist for high-value demand such as BYD Song Plus, BYD Seal, ZEEKR 001, Xiaomi SU7/YU7, Li Auto L7/L9.
- The BYD programming cluster covers service and pain-point intent: diagnostics, updates, BMS, ADAS, multimedia, CarPlay, 12V battery, charging, climate, range loss and model-specific issues.
- Ad landing pages are separated from organic pages with `noindex + canonical`, which avoids duplicate-indexing problems.
- Old moved URLs are preserved with `noindex + canonical` bridge pages.
- Internal SEO links are present in low-noise blocks and footer accordions, so Google can crawl the cluster without making the main funnels feel technical.

## What was fixed

- Canonical URLs were normalized to the final Cloudflare Pages URL shape.
- `.html` URLs were removed from canonical/hreflang/sitemap/internal links.
- Directory pages keep trailing slashes, matching live Cloudflare redirects.
- Sitemap URLs were checked live after deploy: no sitemap URL required a redirect.
- Project documentation now matches the real SEO decision for paid landing pages.

## Recommendations

1. Submit `https://evline.com.ua/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console after each major SEO page batch.
2. Watch Search Console for duplicate canonical, alternate page and hreflang warnings.
3. Enrich the most valuable pages first instead of mass-creating thin pages:
   - `/zapchastyny-byd/song-plus/`
   - `/zapchastyny-zeekr/001/`
   - `/zapchastyny-xiaomi/su7/`
   - `/byd-ne-zaryadzhayetsya/`
   - `/bms-byd-batareya/`
   - `/problemy-byd-song-plus/`
4. Add real cases, photos, before/after notes and pricing examples where possible. This is more valuable than adding many near-identical pages.
5. Keep Google Ads landing pages out of sitemap while they are canonicalized to organic pages.
6. Create or verify Google Business Profile for local queries like `програмування BYD Київ`, `діагностика BYD Київ`, `оновлення BYD Київ`.

## Current risk level

Low. The technical SEO foundation is solid. The main remaining work is content depth, Search Console monitoring and real proof assets.
